Our Mission


Advocacy 1028 is a student-led project created to raise awareness about important advocacy issues and encourage meaningful public discussion. Our goal is to use research, writing, and design to help students communicate ideas that matter and advocate for change in a way that is clear, ethical, and accessible.

This website serves as a platform for student voices, bringing together different perspectives on social, cultural, and policy-related issues that affect real people and communities.


Why This Work Matters

Many advocacy issues are complex, misunderstood, or ignored altogether. Advocacy 1028 exists to help bridge that gap by making research-based advocacy more approachable to a public audience. By presenting information clearly and thoughtfully, we aim to support informed conversations rather than oversimplified arguments.

In a time where misinformation spreads quickly, we believe it is important for students to practice responsible advocacy that is grounded in evidence, empathy, and awareness of audience.

Our Commitment

We are committed to producing advocacy work that is respectful, research-informed, and purposeful. Advocacy 1028 prioritizes clarity, accessibility, and ethical communication, while encouraging students to think critically about the impact of their words and design choices.

Through this project, we hope to empower students to participate in public advocacy and contribute to conversations that promote understanding, awareness, and meaningful change.
