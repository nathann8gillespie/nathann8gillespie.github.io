Social Media

The issue our team is addressing covers a broad topic of social media usage. All of our advocacy posts relate back to social media and the users. The issues we discuss are: usage and its affects on society, impacts that social media holds over users, and political censorship on social media. The changes we are advocating for are: lower usage, not letting social media affect their self worth, and freedom of speech on platforms. Readers can find Instagram and TikTok posts in our section, creating a collection of social media posts to advocate for change that our audience can find easy to read and understand.

Society's Affects on Social Media

This twitter post will demonstrate visuals and wordings on how social media usage has become such a huge part of user's daily routine. Severe amount of usage has caused some negative and positive habits within them which has been a cause for their daily habits around other individuals. Which is where I come in to open the user's eyes and see how using social media can really have an affect on their behaviors without realizing how much of an affect it has caused them.

Affects of Social Media in Relationships

This TikTok uses movie clips to show common unhealthy patterns that are seen in modern relationships, showing patterns like: manipulation, ghosting, non-exclusivity, no effort, cheating, hookup culture, etc. Then after showing these clips it transitions to movie clips that show what healthy, supportive, effort, and genuine love in relationships. The difference in these clips show that although social media connects people it weakens relationships with unhealthy patterns and loss of genuine relationships. The TikToks message is supposed to show what authentic relationships look like when the partners are present, honest, and connected to eachother.
